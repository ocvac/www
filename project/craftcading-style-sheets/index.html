Craftcading Style Sheets

Aka MCSS

Functionality

Minecraft fonts.

Variable-based configuration.

Human readable code, minimal nesting.

Accurate Minecraft colors.

Based on normalize.css

Usage

In your css, add:

@import url("https://ocv.ac/mcss")

or

@import url("https://www.ocvac.net/asset/css/minecraft/global.css")
